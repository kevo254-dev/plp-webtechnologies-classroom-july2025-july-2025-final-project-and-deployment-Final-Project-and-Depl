About Li Rui Construction Limited

History & Philosophy

Li Rui Systems began in 2016 as a general works contractor. Over the years we evolved into Li Rui Construction Limited — taking on design-and-build roles, project management and medium scale main contracts while providing engineering inputs and value-add services.

We focus on clear communication, detailed scheduling, strict supervision, and on-time delivery.

Vision

To be a respectable building contractor delivering beyond expectation, always.

Mission

To procure projects at competitive pricing, provide safe working conditions and deliver quality.

Management & People

Driven by four working directors, our team is continually trained and accredited to remain relevant and deliver professional results.

Accreditation

We are certified by the National Construction Authority (NCA) to operate in Kenya as a road and building works contractor.
